About

I am a mostly self taught 3D hard surface artist, aspiring to be so professionally since first learning CAD modelling 8 years ago. I have a great interest for weapons modelling, being directly inspired by games such as 'Hunt: Showdown' for their quality of work.

My main drive to learn and improve comes from the other works I see posted on sites such as artstation. Seeing and comparing my work to my peers directly fuels my want to improve. I can contribute uniquely to studios with my knowledge on firearm production and assembly, being able to use this to create much more believable and streamlined experiences. My professional aim is to become a dedicated hard surface artist, preferably in the games industry, and to use my skillset to produce high quality and mechanically sound assets.
